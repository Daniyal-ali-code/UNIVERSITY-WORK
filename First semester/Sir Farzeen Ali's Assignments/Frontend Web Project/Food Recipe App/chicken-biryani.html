Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic one-pot dish that holds a special place in the hearts of food lovers around the world. Originating from the Indian subcontinent, this dish is a harmonious blend of marinated chicken, basmati rice, and an array of fragrant spices such as cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, and bay leaves. Often cooked with caramelized onions, fresh herbs, and a touch of saffron, Chicken Biryani is layered and slow-cooked to perfection, allowing the flavors to meld beautifully. Served with cooling raita or a tangy salad, it is a complete meal thatâ€™s both hearty and indulgent, making it perfect for festive occasions or a luxurious weeknight treat.

prep time

30-40 min.

cook time

45-60 min.

serving

4-6 servings

Tags : Chicken

instructions

step 1

Prepare the rice: Rinse and soak the rice. Drain and cook with 4 cups of water and 1 tablespoon of ghee or oil.

step 2

Marinate the chicken: Mix together the yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, garlic, and ginger. Add the chicken and refrigerate for 30 minutes.

step 3

Prepare the biryani masala: Dry roast the coriander seeds, cumin seeds, cinnamon sticks, cardamom pods, mace, and nutmeg. Grind into a fine powder.

step 4

Cook the chicken: Heat oil in a saucepan and cook the marinated chicken until browned and cooked through.

step 5

Prepare the onion and spice mixture: Heat oil in a saucepan and cook the sliced onions until lightly browned. Add the garlic, ginger, cumin seeds, coriander seeds, turmeric powder, and red chili powder. Cook for 1-2 minutes.

step 6

Assemble the biryani: Create layers of cooked rice, onion and spice mixture, and cooked chicken in a large pot or Dutch oven. Sprinkle biryani masala powder and saffron threads over the top.

step 7

7. Cook the biryani: Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook over low heat for 10-15 minutes.

step 8

Serve: Turn off the heat and let the biryani rest for 5 minutes. Fluff the biryani with a fork and serve hot.

ingredients

For Marinade: - Chicken (500g), yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, lemon juice, salt.
Rice: - Basmati rice (2 cups), cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaf, cumin, salt.
Biryani: - Onions, tomatoes, green chilies, coriander, mint, ghee/oil, saffron, cashews (optional).
Spices: - Cumin, cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, star anise, bay leaves.
A blend of marinated chicken, spiced rice, and aromatic herbs ensures a perfect biryani!
