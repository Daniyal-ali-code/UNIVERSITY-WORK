Nihari

Nihari is a beloved traditional Pakistani stew, known for its rich and spicy flavor. Made with slow-cooked beef or mutton, the dish features a thick, aromatic gravy simmered with a blend of spices such as ginger, garlic, cinnamon, cloves, and cardamom. The slow cooking process tenderizes the meat, making it incredibly soft and flavorful. Nihari is typically enjoyed as a breakfast or brunch dish, often paired with naan or paratha to soak up the flavorful gravy. Garnished with fresh coriander, ginger juliennes, and a squeeze of lime, Nihari offers a comforting and indulgent experience, perfect for special occasions or a hearty meal on a cold day.
