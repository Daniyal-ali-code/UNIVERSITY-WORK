Chicken Korma

Chicken Korma is a rich and flavorful dish that showcases the luxurious flavors of Indian and Mughlai cuisine. Made with tender chicken pieces simmered in a creamy, aromatic gravy of yogurt, nuts, and fragrant spices like cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves, it offers a perfect balance of mild heat and sweetness. The nut paste, often made from cashews or almonds, adds a velvety texture, while saffron and cream elevate its richness, making it a dish fit for festive occasions. Best enjoyed with naan, paratha, or steamed rice, Chicken Korma is a true comfort food that brings warmth and indulgence to the table.

prep time

20-25 min.

cook time

45-60 min.

serving

4-6 servings

instructions

step 1

Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and salt.

step 2

Fry sliced onions until golden brown and blend some into a paste.

step 3

Saut√© whole spices in ghee/oil, then cook the marinated chicken.

step 4

Add onion paste, nut paste, and tomato puree, and cook until well combined.

step 5

Simmer with cream, saffron milk, and ground spices until chicken is tender.

step 6

Garnish with fresh cream or coriander and serve hot.

ingredients

Marinade: - Chicken, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, red chili powder, salt.
Gravy: - Onions, tomatoes, nut paste (cashews/almonds), cream, saffron, sugar (optional).
Spices: - Cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaf, cumin seeds, coriander powder, garam masala, cumin powder, nutmeg (optional).
Cooking Medium: - Ghee or oil.
